My Hobbies

When I'm not working on projects, I enjoy a few different things. I love the challenge and teamwork of online games like Dota 2, League of Legends, and Apex Legends. I also find it incredibly satisfying to work with my hands, whether I'm bringing a mechanical keyboard back to life through soldering or prototyping a new PCB design. And to unwind and stay healthy, I jog several times a week.
